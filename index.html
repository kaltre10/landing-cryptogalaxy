Features

Items

To build your own ships you will need items which you can obtain directly by crafting them yourself by having the factory activated

Materials

The materials will be extracted in mining and exploration and can be refined to obtain other more valuable resources

Ships

There are 3 types of ships, those of mining that are used to extract raw materials within planets, those of exploration that are used to explore anomalies in search of precious resources and those of battle that are used to defend some areas or territories and fight. against other players

Expeditions

To carry out expeditions you must buy an exploration ship, when doing expedition you can get materials randomly, items, refined materials and even complete ships

Planets

Each planet can be unlocked by paying an amount of GM that will be different on each planet, on each planet different types of materials can be mined and depending on the experience level in the destination table in that type of resources you can obtain better mining rewards
